My music making handle is WaveParadigm, and I post most of the music I make to http://waveparadigm.bandcamp.com. I'll also be embedding some of my albums here on this page. Enjoy!

Games++

Games++ is a yearly 10 hour Game Jam hosted by Stevens Professor Jeff Thomson. I opted to only make music for this event, and worked with James Romph on his game "Ricochet" as well as Brian Intile and Jack Farzan on their game "yellowbird6." The event was fantastic and I plan on going back next year.

We Guys OST
